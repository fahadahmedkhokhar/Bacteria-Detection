Clean Your Water

We help to clean Water with AI

Pakistan faces risks of biological terrorism and waterborne diseases due to its strategic location and reliance on neighbouring countries like India and Afghanistan for drinking and irrigation water. Continuous monitoring and assessment of water quality are crucial to protect against diseases caused by pathogens such as bacteria, viruses, and protozoa. Consumers demand non-toxic drinking water that meets quality standards and satisfies visual aspects like color, taste, and odor. Water contamination can potentially lead to bioterrorism by introducing biological warfare agents into natural resources.

Features

Water Contamination Detection

The solution detects whether water is contaminated or not, providing vital information for human survival, especially in regions where clean water is scarce.

Rapid Results

Overcomes the delay time in getting the result of water contamination using traditional methods, which can take up to 5–6 hours. This implies a significant improvement in response time, crucial for addressing water contamination promptly.

Bacteria Detection:

Utilizes a novel deep learning-based model to detect colonies of bacteria in water samples, post gram staining. This approach helps in identifying the type of bacteria present and their respective quantities, providing insights into potential health risks associated with water consumption.

Customizable Parameters

Allows users to specify parameters for the deep learning model, enhancing flexibility and adaptability to different scenarios and datasets.

Comprehensive Testing

Conducts tests using datasets from various researchers to improve the ability to detect a wide range of dangerous bacteria, including E. coli, yeast, and other particles commonly found in contaminated water sources.

Accuracy

Achieves a high accuracy rate of 84.56% on test benchmarks, indicating reliable performance in detecting water contamination and identifying harmful bacteria.

Quantification of Contamination

Provides information on the level of contamination in water, aiding in assessing the severity of the issue and guiding appropriate remedial actions.

About

Traditional methods of water testing often involve laborious processes and can take several hours, sometimes even days, to deliver results. In contrast, our solution overcomes these delays with its rapid detection capabilities.

By leveraging advanced technology, including deep learning-based models, we significantly reduce the time needed to assess water quality, providing results in a fraction of the time, typically within minutes.
